About Us

Gripstone Security (Pvt) Ltd is a fully registered Zimbabwean company specializing in providing top-notch security services. We offer a range of services, including armed and unarmed guards, patrol and inspection, access control, and specialized client-requested services.

Operating throughout Zimbabwe, we cater to diverse customers across various industries and segments. Our protective services, co-created with our clients, incorporate a high degree of technological innovation. This allows us to meet specific customer demands while maintaining a competitive pricing structure.

We have successfully integrated technology, people, and knowledge to deliver protective services across Zimbabwe, offering skilled solutions to meet your security needs.
